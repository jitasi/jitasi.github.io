### Problem
Let $a$ and $b$ be the roots of the equation

$$
x^2 − mx + 2 = 0
$$

Suppose that $a + \frac{1}{b}$ and $b + \frac{1}{a}$ are the roots of the equation

$$
x^2 − px + q = 0
$$.

What is $q$?
