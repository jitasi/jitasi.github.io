### Problem

Floor function is the function that takes as input a real number $x$, and gives as output the greatest integer less than or equal to $x$, denoted $[x]$.

For example:

$$
\begin{align*}
& [3.3] = 3  & [3.8] = 3 \\
& [3] = 3    & [4] = 4 \\
& [-1] = -1  & [-1.2] = -2
\end{align*}
$$

Find the value of $[(\sqrt{7}+\sqrt{5})^6]$
